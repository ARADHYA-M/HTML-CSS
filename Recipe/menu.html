Tomato Basil Pastas

SERVINGS:2

PREPPING TIME:15 MIN

COOKING TIME:30 MIN

Ingredients

200 grams spaghetti

2 tablespoond olive oil

2 cloves garlic,minced

1 can diced tomatoes

1/2 teaspoon salt

1/2 teaspoon pepper

1/2 cup fresh basil leaves,chopped

1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Directions

  • Cook spaghetti according to package instructions,drain,reserving 1 cup of the pasra water.
  • In a skillet,heat olive oil,saute garlic until fragrant,then add tomatoes,salt and pepper.Cook until sauce thickens.
  • Add the cooked spaghetti to the skillet, tossing to combine and adding reserved pasta water if needed.
  • Off the heat,garnish with basil and parmesan.Serve immediately.

    • Tips

    Add a pinch of red pepper flakes for a kick.

    You can substitute spaghetti with any paste of your choice.
